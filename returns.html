01Eligibility

Return requests must be submitted to support within 14 days of delivery and approved by our team before the parcel is sent back. Items must be returned in their original, unopened packaging with all tamper-evident seals intact.

02Non-returnable items

For safety, product integrity, and regulatory reasons, the following are not eligible for return:

  • Opened products (broken seal, removed cap, removed crimp).
  • Used or partially used products.
  • Items with broken tamper-evident integrity.
  • Items returned outside the 14-day window.
  • Items returned without prior approval from support.
This policy exists to protect the integrity of every product in the supply chain. We cannot accept returns of opened research compounds under any circumstances.

03Damaged or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged in transit, or you receive the wrong product, contact support within 48 hours of delivery with:

  • Your order number.
  • Clear photos of the outer parcel, inner packaging, and the product label.
  • A brief description of the issue.

We will review and resolve the issue quickly — typically by sending a replacement at no additional cost, or issuing a full refund where a replacement is not possible.

04Refund method

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times depend on the payment provider:

  • Card payments: 3–5 working days after the refund is issued.
  • Apple Pay / Google Pay: 3–5 working days.

You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been issued.

05Return shipping

Where the return is our error (damaged, defective or incorrect item), reasonable return shipping costs are covered by UKMAXX. In all other cases, return shipping is the customer's responsibility. We recommend using a tracked, insured service for any return — UKMAXX is not responsible for returns lost in transit.

06Process & timing

Once your return is approved by support:

  1. You will receive a return reference and the return shipping address.
  2. Send the parcel back within 7 days and reply to support with the tracking number.
  3. Once received and inspected, we will issue the refund within 3 working days.
  4. Your card statement may take an additional 3–5 working days to reflect the refund.

Questions? Contact our support team with your order number and we'll help.
