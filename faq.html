FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything about ordering, shipping, verification and handling. All products are supplied strictly for laboratory and in-vitro research use only.

Orders & Payments

How quickly do you dispatch?
Orders placed before 2PM (UK time) are usually dispatched same day. Orders after 2PM typically dispatch next working day.
What payment methods do you accept?
Checkout is processed securely via Stripe. Available methods are shown at checkout based on your device and location.
Can I change or cancel an order?
If your order has not been dispatched yet, contact support as soon as possible with your order reference and requested change.

Shipping & Delivery

Do you ship from the UK?
Yes. Stock is held in the UK to reduce delivery times and avoid customs delays for UK deliveries.
How long does delivery take?
Most UK deliveries arrive in 1–2 working days after dispatch. Times may vary during peak periods or courier disruptions.
Is shipping discreet?
Yes. Parcels are packed discreetly and securely for standard courier handling.

Batch Verification & COA

Are batches independently tested?
Yes. Batches are tested by third-party labs and associated COA information is published for verification.
How do I verify my batch?
Use the batch code/QR on your vial and compare it against the published lab result details (sample, method, date, purity and lab).
What if I cannot find my batch code?
Contact support with a clear photo of the label and your order details so the team can assist with traceability checks.

Product Handling

How should products be stored?
Follow the storage guidance shown in product specs. As a general rule, keep lyophilised compounds cold and protected from heat/light.
Do you provide usage or dosing advice?
No. UKMAXX does not provide medical, human-use or dosing advice. Products are supplied for research use only.
Can I return opened items?
Opened or used items are generally not eligible for return due to safety and integrity requirements. See the Returns policy for full terms.

Legal & Compliance

Are products for human consumption?
No. All products are sold strictly for laboratory and in-vitro research use only.
Do you verify customer age?
By purchasing, customers confirm they are legally permitted to buy and possess research compounds under applicable law.
Need help with an existing order? Include your order reference and batch code (if applicable) when contacting support for fastest resolution.
