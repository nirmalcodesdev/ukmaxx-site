Frequently asked questions

Everything about ordering, shipping, batch verification and handling. All products are supplied strictly for laboratory and in-vitro research use only.

Orders & Payments 3

How quickly do you dispatch?

Orders placed before 2PM (UK time) are usually dispatched same working day. Orders after 2PM typically dispatch the next working day. You will receive a dispatch confirmation with tracking by email.

What payment methods do you accept?

Checkout is processed securely via Stripe. Available methods (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay, Google Pay) are shown at checkout based on your device and location.

Can I change or cancel an order?

If your order has not yet been dispatched, contact support as soon as possible with your order reference and requested change. Once an order has been dispatched it cannot be amended, but you may refuse delivery under our returns policy.

Shipping & Delivery 3

Do you ship from the UK?

Yes. All stock is held in the UK to minimise delivery times and avoid customs delays for UK and EU customers.

How long does delivery take?

Most UK deliveries arrive in 1–2 working days after dispatch. EU destinations typically take 3–5 working days. Times may vary during peak periods or due to courier disruption.

Is shipping discreet?

Yes. All parcels are packed in plain, unbranded outer packaging for standard courier handling. The contents are not described on the outside of the parcel.

Batch Verification & COA 3

Are batches independently tested?

Yes. Every production batch is tested by a third-party laboratory (HPLC, UPLC/MS or NMR) and the associated Certificate of Analysis is published for verification.

How do I verify my batch?

Use the batch code printed on your vial label and compare it against the published lab result details (sample, method, date, purity, lab). If anything does not match, contact support immediately.

What if I cannot find my batch code?

Contact support with a clear photo of the label and your order details. The team can run a traceability check and confirm the batch against our records.

Product Handling 3

How should products be stored?

Follow the storage guidance shown in the product specification sheet. As a general rule, keep lyophilised compounds cold, dry and protected from light. Avoid repeated freeze–thaw cycles after reconstitution.

Do you provide usage or dosing advice?

No. UKMAXX does not provide medical, human-use, or dosing advice of any kind. All products are supplied strictly for laboratory and in-vitro research use only.

Can I return opened items?

Opened or used items are generally not eligible for return due to safety and integrity requirements. See the full Returns policy for details.

Legal & Compliance 2

Are products for human consumption?

No. All products are sold strictly for laboratory and in-vitro research purposes only. They are not intended for human or animal use, and are not medicines, food supplements, or cosmetics.

Do you verify customer age?

Yes. The site is gated to users aged 18 or older. By purchasing, customers confirm they are legally permitted to buy and possess research compounds under the laws of their jurisdiction.
