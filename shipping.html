Orders placed before 2PM (UK time) are typically dispatched same working day. Orders after cut-off dispatch next working day.

Tracking information is provided once available from the carrier.

4. Address Accuracy

Customers are responsible for accurate shipping details. UKMAXX is not responsible for delays caused by incorrect or incomplete addresses.

5. Failed Delivery / Return to Sender

If a parcel is returned to sender, re-shipping fees may apply.

6. International Shipping

If offered, customers are responsible for checking local regulations and any import requirements.
