Shipping policy

How we dispatch, how long delivery takes, tracking, and what to do if something goes wrong.

Same-day dispatch on orders before 2PM UK time · Tracked 24 as standard

01Dispatch times

Orders placed before 2PM (UK time) are typically dispatched the same working day. Orders placed after 2PM, or over a weekend / public holiday, dispatch on the next working day.

Cut-off: 14:00 UK time, Mon–Fri (excluding UK public holidays). Orders after cut-off are treated as next-working-day.

02Delivery windows

Most UK shipments arrive within 1–2 working days after dispatch via Royal Mail Tracked 24 or equivalent next-day service. EU destinations typically take 3–5 working days. Delays may occur during peak periods (Black Friday, Christmas) or due to courier disruption, weather events or local customs processing.

03Tracking

Tracking information is emailed to you once the carrier has scanned the parcel into their network — usually the same evening as dispatch, or first thing the next working day. Tracking links are valid on the carrier's website and on your UKMAXX order page.

04Address accuracy

Customers are responsible for ensuring the shipping address provided at checkout is accurate and complete. UKMAXX is not responsible for delays or losses caused by incorrect or incomplete addresses. If you spot an address error, contact support within 1 hour of placing the order and we will attempt to amend it before dispatch.

05Failed delivery & return to sender

If a parcel cannot be delivered and is returned to sender, we will contact you to arrange redelivery. Re-shipping fees apply in this case to cover the second delivery. We will not re-ship until the re-shipping fee is paid.

06International shipping

We currently ship to the United Kingdom, Ireland, Germany, France and the Netherlands. Where international shipping is offered, customers are responsible for checking local regulations and any import requirements before ordering. UKMAXX is not responsible for parcels refused, held or destroyed by customs in the destination country.

07Lost or damaged parcels

If your parcel is delayed beyond 5 UK working days after dispatch, or arrives damaged, contact support with your order number. We will open a trace with the carrier and, where appropriate, send a replacement or issue a refund. We may ask for a photo of the damaged parcel and contents.
