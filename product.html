Common questions

Is this product tested before dispatch?

Yes. Every UKMAXX batch is independently tested by a third-party laboratory (typically Janoshik Analytical) before it's released for sale. The COA certificate is accessible via the QR code printed on every vial label.

How is the product shipped?

All orders ship from UK stock via Royal Mail Tracked 24. Orders placed before 2PM (UK time) on a working day are dispatched the same day in insulated, tamper-evident packaging. Tracking is sent by email once dispatched.

What is the storage requirement?

Lyophilised (freeze-dried) peptide vials should be stored at -20°C until first use. After reconstitution with bacteriostatic water, store at 2–8°C and use within 28 days. Detailed storage and reconstitution instructions are included with every order.

Can I return the product?

For research integrity reasons, we can only accept returns of unopened and factory-sealed products within 30 days of purchase. Once a vial's seal is broken, the product cannot be restocked or resold. See our full returns policy for details.
