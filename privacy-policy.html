PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: 25 May 2026. UKMAXX is operated by Octa Technologies Ltd. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details (name, email)
  • Order and delivery details (address, postcode, country)
  • Transaction metadata required for payment processing
  • Basic technical data (IP, browser/device info, usage logs)

2. How We Use Information

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Provide delivery and customer support updates
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal and accounting obligations

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed via Stripe. UKMAXX does not store full card numbers. Payment data handling is subject to Stripe’s own privacy and security controls.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only where necessary with service providers (e.g., payment processors and couriers) and where required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain order and accounting records for as long as reasonably required for legal, tax, and operational purposes, then securely delete or anonymise data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Contact support to make a request.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect your data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Contact

For privacy requests, contact UKMAXX support and include relevant order details where applicable.
