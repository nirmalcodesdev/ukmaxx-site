01Information we collect

UKMAXX is operated by Octa Technologies Ltd ("we", "us"). This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you browse our website or place an order.

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact details — your name, email address and phone number (where provided).
  • Order & delivery details — billing and shipping address, postcode and country.
  • Transaction metadata — required for payment processing (handled by Stripe; see section 3).
  • Basic technical data — IP address, browser/device info and anonymised usage logs.
  • Communications — any messages you send to our support team.

02How we use information

We use your data only for the purposes set out below:

  • Process and fulfil your orders, including dispatch and delivery.
  • Provide customer support and respond to enquiries.
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmation, dispatch, delivery).
  • Send optional marketing emails (only with your consent — see section 6).
  • Prevent fraud, abuse and security incidents.
  • Comply with legal, tax and accounting obligations.
Lawful basis. We process your data on the basis of contract performance (fulfilling your order), legitimate interest (security, fraud prevention), legal obligation (tax/records) and consent (marketing emails).

03Payment processing

All payments are processed by Stripe Payments Europe Ltd. UKMAXX does not store, see or transmit full card numbers. Payment data handling is subject to Stripe's privacy policy and PCI-DSS controls.

We receive limited transaction metadata (last 4 digits, brand, country, status) for accounting and customer support purposes only.

04Data sharing

We share personal data only where strictly necessary to deliver our service:

  • Payment processors — Stripe (payments, fraud signals).
  • Couriers — name, address, email and phone (where required for delivery).
  • Email service — Resend (transactional and, with consent, marketing emails).
  • Hosting & analytics — Vercel (hosting) and Supabase (data store, EU region).
  • Legal — where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

We never sell your personal data.

05Data retention

We retain order and accounting records for as long as reasonably required for legal, tax and operational purposes (typically 6 years for UK tax records), then securely delete or anonymise the data. Marketing subscription data is retained until you unsubscribe.

06Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (UK GDPR / EU GDPR / similar), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities.
  • Data portability for data you have provided to us.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g. unsubscribe from marketing emails).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g. the ICO in the UK).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in section 9. We respond within 30 days.

07Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect your data, including HTTPS in transit, encryption at rest in our databases, access controls, and least-privilege principles for staff. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we continuously review and improve our controls.

08Cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies: essential cookies (cart, age verification) and, with your consent, basic analytics cookies. You can manage cookie preferences via the banner shown on your first visit.

09Contact

For any privacy-related request (access, deletion, unsubscribe, complaint), contact our team:

  • Email: support@ukmaxx.co.uk
  • Operator: Octa Technologies Ltd, United Kingdom
  • Response time: within 30 days
